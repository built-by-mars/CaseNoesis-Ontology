                                      INTRODUCTION

       At all times material to this Third Superseding Indictment:

       1.     (01) BRYAN C. PERRY (PERRY) was a resident of Clarksville, Tennessee.

       2.     (02) JONATHAN S. O’DELL (O’DELL) was a resident of Warsaw, Benton

County, Missouri, within the Western District of Missouri.

       3.     The defendants were members of the “2nd American Militia.”

Washington, D.C. area, “[t]o stop the madness going on.”
